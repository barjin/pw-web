PWWW Selector Playground

This webpage is intended for selector generator testing. It contains various elements and DOM structures to bring out the best out of the current implementation of selector generator (located in ./build/extractSelector.ts). Just click anywhere on the page and check console logs!

Links

There might be a link, a link with a twist, and another link.

Both first two links are anchor links (leading to the same page), differing in text and the second one having an onclick attribute, inducing a JS action. The third link's text content is identical to the first one, however, this one leads somewhere else.
